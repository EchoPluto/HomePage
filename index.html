Biography

Hi, I'm Hengjia Li (李恒佳). I am currently a final-year Ph.D. student in the State Key Lab of CAD&CG at Zhejiang University guided by Pro. Deng Cai and Pro. Xiaofei He. Before this, I received my B.Eng. in Mathematical Sciences of Zhejiang University.

I have a broad interest in most areas of computer vision, especially in image/video generation and multi-modal models with a recent focus on post training. Please do not hesitate to reach out to me via email at lihengjia98@gmail.com !
